Grimoire Weiss...

Posted June 1st, 3456

After telling Yonah about the Lunar Tears yesterday before sleep, I couldn't find her anywhere this morning.
I've asked Devola, granny near the fountain, every vendor on the shopping district. No one knew where she went
Thankfully Popola pointed me to the Lost Shrine to the east, so I rushed there.
It was desolate and full of Shades, yet the top room had 2 huge statues, they turned out to be Shades. Also, between them was a single floating book, stationary, as if it was hanged from the ceiling. It spoke to me and lent me it's power, said it's name was Grimoire Weiss or something, so I called him Weiss, to which he didn't react lightly.
Killed the Shades, proceeded forth and there was Yonah, semi-conscious. Took her home, made her dinner. All is well
