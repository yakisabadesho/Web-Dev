Top 3 foods to try in Japan:

  1. Ramen
    An evolved form of the chinese lamian, ramen is a nourishing, succulent noodle dish, that can be served with any topping and meat you have in your fridge:
  2. Takoyaki
    A ball-shaped snack, made out of wheat based batter and cooked in a special molded pan, that gives it the signature spherical shape. Typically filled with diced or minced octopus and vegetables to taste:
  3. Yakisoba
    Stir-fried noodles with meat and vegetables, similar to suiru lagman
